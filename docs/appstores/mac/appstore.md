LogoRRR: The Interactive Log Analyzer

Stop searching, start seeing. LogoRRR is a powerful, high-performance log analysis tool designed to transform massive, incomprehensible text files into clear, actionable visual data. Whether you are a developer debugging a complex crash or a system administrator monitoring server health, LogoRRR makes identifying recurring patterns and critical events effortless and efficient.

Why LogoRRR?

Its unique visual approach allows you to "see" the structure of your logs at a glance, making it the ultimate "microscope" for your data.

Key Features:

- Performance: Optimized for macOS, LogoRRR opens and searches large log files instantly.
- Visual Pattern Recognition: See your log file as a dense visualization where colors and shapes represent different log levels and search hits. Spot anomalies in seconds.
- Customizable Search Groups: Create, save, and reuse your favorite filter queries. Mark groups as "Global" to access them across all your projects or keep them file-specific.
- Enhanced Search Visuals: Every search term includes an integrated hit counter, showing the frequency of occurrences at a glance.
- Real-Time Log Tailing: Monitor active log files as they grow with a smooth, reimplemented "tail -f" functionality.
- Smart Time-Filtering: Automatically detect timestamps and use an intuitive slider to focus on specific time periods within your logs
- Favourites & Settings: Save your most-used patterns as Favourites. LogoRRR remembers your last accessed files, directories, and active filters, so you can pick up right where you left off.
- Zip & Directory Support: Drag and drop entire directories or compressed .zip files. LogoRRR handles the heavy lifting without requiring manual extraction.

Perfect for:

- Software Developers: Quickly filter stack traces and debug application behavior.
- DevOps & SysAdmins: Monitor real-time logs and track down server errors across multiple files.
- Data Analysts: Identify recurring patterns and trends in any structured or unstructured text file.

Built with Passion

LogoRRR is an open-source project dedicated to making the lives of developers easier. By combining the power of Scala with a modern JavaFX UI, it provides a cross-platform experience that doesn't compromise on performance or usability.
